Alluri Bapineedu and Pendyala Ranga Rao Degree and PG College came into existence in 1999 by AVR Trust. The degree and PG college is affiliated to the Adikavi Nannaya University, Rajahmundry. The college offers B.Sc. program in eight disciplines, B.Com. in two disciplines and M.Sc. in two disciplines at the UG and PG level. The placement cell at the college plays a crucial role in finding job opportunities for final year UG & PG students by keeping in touch with reputed organizations and industries. A total of 130 students are placed in top companies during the financial year 2019-20. Some of the top recruiters that recruit students from this college include Infosys, TCS, Wipro, Capgemini, Hetero Pharma, Laurus Labs etc.
